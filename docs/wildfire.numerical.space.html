wildfire.numerical.space.diffmat module¶

Space derivatives approximation using differentiation matrices

Implements differentiation matrices for:

  • Finite Differences of second and fourth order of accuracy

  • Chebyshev differentiation matrix

Details in: Trefethen, L. N. (2000). Spectral methods in MATLAB. Society for industrial and applied mathematics. https://doi.org/10.1137/1.9780898719598
